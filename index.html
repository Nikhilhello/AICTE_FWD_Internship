Smart Study Planner

About the Platform

Smart Study Planner is a digital tool designed to help students organize, track, and complete their study tasks efficiently. It focuses on productivity, consistency, and visualization of tasks to help you manage your learning schedule effectively.

Sections Explained

1. Add New Task

  • Use this section to add your study tasks.
  • Enter the task name, subject, and due date.
  • Helps you plan what to study and when.

2. Progress

  • Shows your overall progress based on completed tasks.
  • Visual representation helps you understand how much work is done and pending.

3. Your Tasks

  • Displays all your added tasks.
  • Tasks can be sorted by deadline or subject.
  • You can mark tasks as complete, edit details, or delete tasks.
  • Ensures all tasks are in one organized list for easy management.

4. Streak Tracker

  • Tracks your weekly streaks of completed tasks.
  • Helps motivate consistent studying by showing consecutive days of task completion.

5. Task Timeline

  • Shows a chronological list of tasks.
  • Helps you see which tasks are upcoming or completed at a glance.

Getting Started – Step by Step

  1. Click “Add New Task” and enter your study activities.
  2. Check Progress to track completed tasks.
  3. Use Your Tasks to edit, delete, or mark tasks complete.
  4. Monitor your Streak to stay consistent.
  5. Refer to Task Timeline to view all upcoming and completed tasks.

Highlights / Key Benefits

  • Today's tasks will trigger alerts/pop-ups to remind you.
  • Encourages consistent studying with streak tracking.
  • Helps prioritize and organize tasks by subject or deadline.
  • Provides a visual overview of all tasks and completion progress.
  • Makes task management simple, interactive, and motivating.
